FPT About Us

FPT describes itself as a leading technology corporation in Vietnam, a pioneer in technology, and a company aiming to affirm Vietnam's position on the global ICT map.

Key figures shown on the About Us page at crawl time: 38 years since establishment, operations in 30 countries, VND 70,113 billion total revenue in 2025, and 54,110 employees.

FPT is a pioneer in digital transformation and a leader in consulting, providing and deploying technology and telecommunications services and solutions. The company helps clients in 30 countries and regions realize technology-driven business development strategies and goals.

With over three decades of experience implementing projects on a global scale, FPT helps clients overcome challenges and achieve performance in their digital transformation journeys. Based on technologies including AI, Big Data, Cloud, Automation and IoT, FPT offers advanced technology solutions and services to help customers operate proactively and flexibly.
