FPT Corporation

The FPT English homepage presents FPT as a leading Vietnamese technology corporation with core activities in Technology and Education. The page highlights FPT's ecosystem of services, products, solutions and Made-by-FPT platforms that support sustainable growth for organizations and distinctive experiences for customers.

Featured news includes FPT's partnership with Vietnam's Ministry of Justice to apply digital technologies and AI in nationwide legal document review; the FPT Chairman's statement that AI is rewriting the operating system for all organizations; the release of FPT Annual Report 2025; the Au Lac Grand Prize for Vietnamese AI; and FPT's deployment of a smart operations center for the Civil Judgment Enforcement Authority.

Strategic vision: FPT's long-term goal is to become a digital enterprise and stand in the top 50 global leading end-to-end digital transformation solutions and services providers by 2030.

Action areas include Business, Technology and People. FPT invests in and develops comprehensive digital transformation services and solutions for customers of all sizes and fields. FPT updates the latest technology trends to develop core technologies and platforms that improve customer experience and operational efficiency. FPT commits to a fair, transparent, diversified and inclusive working environment.

A Made-by-FPT technology ecosystem helps businesses and organizations accelerate digital transformation, improve productivity, save costs and maximize resources.

FPT ecosystem: Technology, Education and Member Companies. FPT is an important strategic partner in consulting, training and implementing digital transformation for leading organizations globally and in Vietnam. FPT Education expands across education levels to provide high-quality human resources.

Sustainable development: ESG orientations and priorities are integral to FPT's development strategy and reflect the corporation's responsibilities to stakeholders.

Investor relations: the homepage links to general meetings of shareholders, financial reports, annual reports and earnings reports. The stock information section displayed a closing price of VND 75,500 as of May 1, 2026.
